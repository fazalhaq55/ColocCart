# 🛒 ColocCart: Smart Micro-Pooling for Students

> **Proactive Micro-Pooling & Shared Grocery Shopping**

ColocCart is a hyper-local Android application designed to connect university students before they shop, unlocking bulk discounts and fostering real-world community connections. Built as an engineering project for the Software Engineering and Digital Transformation program at ESIGELEC.

## 🎯 The Problem: The "Bulk Penalty"
Students often want to buy in bulk to save money, but lack the upfront budget or storage space to do it alone. Existing solutions like Splitwise only settle debts *after* the purchase, and WhatsApp groups quickly become chaotic when trying to organize logistics and finances.

## 💡 The Solution
ColocCart provides a dedicated platform to securely connect strangers in the same campus hub (like CROUS Madrillet) *before* a purchase is made. 

## ✨ Core Features
*   **📍 Campus Hub Detection:** Automatically restricts the feed strictly to your local building or campus hub to ensure you are pooling with nearby neighbors.
*   **🛒 Proactive Micro-Pooling:** Create open carts for bulk items (cleaning supplies, a shared printer, groceries) and set the total slots and cost.
*   **💬 Integrated Group Chat:** A secure, private in-app messaging system tied directly to your specific pool.
